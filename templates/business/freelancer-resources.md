## üí∞ Pricing Strategies

### Hourly Rates by Experience
- **Junior (0-2 years):** $25-50/hr
- **Mid-Level (2-5 years):** $50-100/hr
- **Senior (5+ years):** $100-200/hr
- **Expert (10+ years):** $200-400/hr

### Value-Based Pricing
- Price based on client value, not hours
- Focus on ROI for client
- Higher rates, better clients

### Project-Based Pricing
- Fixed scope, fixed price
- Clearer expectations
- Less hourly tracking

### Retainer Agreements
- Monthly recurring revenue
- Predictable income
- Long-term relationships

## üí° Pro Tips

1. **Always use contracts** - Protect yourself legally
2. **50% upfront payment** - Minimum for new clients
3. **Track everything** - Time, expenses, communications
4. **Build an emergency fund** - 3-6 months expenses
5. **Set boundaries** - Don't work 24/7
6. **Raise rates regularly** - Annual increases
7. **Collect testimonials** - Social proof matters
8. **Specialize** - Niche expertise commands higher rates
9. **Network consistently** - Relationships = business
10. **Invest in yourself** - Education and tools

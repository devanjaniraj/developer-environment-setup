## üöÄ Quick Start Guide

### For New Freelancers

1. **Set Up Your Business**
   - Read `freelancer-resources.md` for tool recommendations
   - Choose accounting software (Wave, FreshBooks)
   - Set up payment processing (PayPal, Stripe)

2. **Create Your Contract**
   - Customize `contract-template.md` with your details
   - Have a lawyer review it (one-time investment)
   - Use for every project

3. **Prepare Proposal Template**
   - Customize `proposal-template.md` with your branding
   - Update technology stack with your expertise
   - Adjust pricing based on your rates

4. **Set Up Time Tracking**
   - Use `time-tracking-template.md` or digital tools (Toggl, Harvest)
   - Track all billable hours
   - Review weekly for productivity insights

5. **Create Invoice Template**
   - Customize `invoice-template.md` with your details
   - Add your payment information
   - Send invoices promptly upon milestone completion

- **Mid-Level (2-5 years):** $50-100/hr
- **Senior (5+ years):** $100-200/hr
- **Expert (10+ years):** $200-400/hr

### Project-Based Pricing
Calculate: `Total Hours √ó Hourly Rate √ó 0.9` (10% discount for fixed price)

### Retainer Pricing
- **Basic Support:** $500-1,000/month (5-10 hours)
- **Standard Retainer:** $2,000-5,000/month (20-40 hours)
- **Premium Retainer:** $5,000-10,000/month (40-80 hours)

## üõ°Ô∏è Best Practices

### Always Do This ‚úÖ
1. **Use contracts** - Every single project, no exceptions
2. **Request 40-50% upfront** - Protects your time investment
3. **Track all hours** - Even on fixed-price projects
4. **Send invoices promptly** - Don't wait weeks
5. **Document everything** - Emails, decisions, changes
6. **Set clear boundaries** - Working hours, response times
7. **Communicate proactively** - Weekly updates minimum
8. **Collect testimonials** - After every successful project
9. **Raise rates annually** - 10-20% per year
10. **Build emergency fund** - 3-6 months of expenses

### Never Do This ‚ùå
1. ‚ùå Start work without a contract
2. ‚ùå Work for free (except strategic opportunities)
3. ‚ùå Skip the deposit
4. ‚ùå Miss deadlines without communication
5. ‚ùå Ignore scope creep
6. ‚ùå Undervalue your work
7. ‚ùå Work 24/7 (burnout is real)
8. ‚ùå Chase bad clients
9. ‚ùå Forget to invoice
10. ‚ùå Neglect taxes (set aside 25-30%)

## üí° Pro Tips

### Pricing Strategy
- Start conservative, raise rates every 6 months
- Charge more for rush jobs (1.5-2x rate)
- Offer package deals for multiple projects
- Consider value-based pricing for experienced freelancers

### Client Management
- Set clear working hours (9am-6pm)
- Use project management tools (Trello, Notion)
- Weekly check-ins keep clients happy
- Underpromise and overdeliver

### Tax Management
- Set aside 25-30% of income for taxes
- Track ALL expenses (software, equipment, internet)
- Make quarterly estimated tax payments
- Consider hiring a CPA once you hit $50K revenue

### Scaling Your Business
- Document your processes
- Hire contractors for overflow work
- Create passive income (courses, templates)
- Build productized services
- Focus on high-value clients
